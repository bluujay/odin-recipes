Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

GOD I LOVE THESE. Balsamic vinegar adds a punch of flavor to these easy roasted Brussels sprouts, while the Parmesan provides a melty, cheesy finish.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp ground plack pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes, or to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Toss Brussels sprouts with olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt. Spread onto a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven, shaking every 5 minutes, until crispy and fork-tender, about 20 minutes. Toss with balsamic vinegar and sprinkle with Parmesan. Continue roasting until melted, about 1 minute more. Sprinkle with red pepper.
